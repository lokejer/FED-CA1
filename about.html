Hi! I'm Jerome.

My passion lies in living a more eco-friendly, mindful life. Over the past few years, I’ve been on a journey to reduce my waste and live more sustainably—one small habit at a time. From composting food scraps to switching to reusable everyday items, I’ve learned that even the tiniest changes can make a big difference.

One of my favourite “green hacks” is making my own all-purpose cleaner using vinegar, citrus peels, and a few drops of essential oil—it smells amazing and cuts down on plastic waste! I also love upcycling old clothes and finding creative ways to give things a second life before tossing them out.

For me, eco-living isn’t about being perfect—it’s about being intentional and constantly improving. I'm excited to share what I’ve learned and keep exploring ways to make sustainable living more accessible, fun, and rewarding.
